The Cork Walls

The DYI home sustainable insulator

Our Mission

At The Cork Walls, our mission is to provide affordable, sustainable home insulation solutions using one of Portugal's most abundant and eco-friendly resources—cork. We believe that everyone deserves a comfortable, energy-efficient home, regardless of their financial situation. By offering DIY cork insulation kits, we empower middle-aged and elderly homeowners, especially vulnerable families, to take control of their energy consumption and reduce their heating costs. Our goal is to combat energy poverty in Portugal, and across Europe, by making home insulation simple, accessible, and eco-friendly.

Why Cork?

Cork is a natural, renewable material that has been used for centuries in Portugal. It is harvested without damaging trees, making it one of the most sustainable materials available. Not only is cork an excellent insulator, but it also contributes to reducing carbon emissions, making your home more energy-efficient while helping the environment. By choosing cork, you are investing in a greener future for yourself and future generations.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Eco-Friendly Materials: All of our products are made from natural cork, harvested sustainably in Portugal. We prioritize the health of our planet while providing practical insulation solutions.

Energy Efficiency: Our cork insulation can significantly reduce heating and cooling costs, helping to lower your energy bills and decrease your environmental footprint.

Support Local Communities: By sourcing and manufacturing locally, we support local economies and create jobs within our community.

How We Work

Step-by-Step DIY Kits: Our insulation kits come with everything you need to start your home project—from cork panels to tools and guides. We make it easy for anyone to install cork insulation without hiring expensive contractors.

Customer Support: Our team is available to guide you through the installation process and answer any questions. Whether you need assistance choosing the right kit or help with installation, we are here for you.

Affordable Solutions: We believe insulation should be accessible to everyone. That's why we offer competitive pricing and aim to make our kits affordable for families struggling with energy poverty.
